# am.

## what is am?

**am** = **the first-person singular present-tense form of the verb "to be."**  
it links "i" to a current identity, condition, or role.

---

## why am matters

- it expresses **present state**  
- it functions as a **linking verb** between subject and descriptor  
- it turns labels into explicit self-claims when paired with "i"

---

## am summary

**am = present-tense being link for first-person statements.**
