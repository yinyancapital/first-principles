# i.

## what is i?

**i** = **the first-person singular pronoun used by a speaker to refer to self.**  
it marks who is talking or claiming.

---

## why i matters

- it sets the **agent** of a sentence  
- it anchors perspective to the **self**  
- it helps map responsibility, intent, and experience

---

## i summary

**i = self-reference token for the speaker.**
