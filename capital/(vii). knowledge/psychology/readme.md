# psychology.

## start with the basic truths
i. **behavior has causes.**  
   thoughts, emotions, biology, and context all matter.

ii. **perception is filtered.**  
   people do not experience reality as raw data.

iii. **habits and patterns repeat.**  
   repeated cues and rewards shape future action.

iv. **the mind is adaptive but biased.**  
   mental shortcuts help speed but can distort judgment.

---

## what is psychology?

**psychology** = **the study of mind and behavior, including how people think, feel, learn, and act.**

---

## psychology breakdown

1. **cognition**  
   attention, memory, reasoning, and decision processes.

2. **emotion and motivation**  
   affective states and drives that energize behavior.

3. **learning and conditioning**  
   how experience changes future responses.

4. **personality and differences**  
   stable tendencies that vary across people.

5. **social influence**  
   how groups, norms, and relationships affect choices.

6. **mental health and regulation**  
   mechanisms of stress, resilience, and well-being.

---

## a simple example

you plan to study but keep checking your phone:
- cue: notification sound
- reward: instant novelty
- behavior repeats automatically

psychology explains this as a **cue-reward learning loop**.

---

## psychology summary

**psychology = how minds work and why behavior happens.**
