### Start with the basic truths
1. **People do more together than alone.**  
2. **Trust makes cooperation possible.**  
3. **Cooperation creates new value.**

---

### What are relationships (first principles)?

**Relationships = connections between people that allow trust and teamwork.**

They can be:
- Family
- Friends
- Mentors
- Teammates
- Communities

---

### How relationships connect to capital

- Relationships create **access** to resources.
- They help you find **opportunities** and **support**.

So:
- **Relationships are social capital.**

---

### How relationships connect to knowledge and skills

- You can **learn faster** from others.
- Others can **teach** or **mentor** you.

---

### How relationships connect to time and money

- Good relationships can **save time** (help, advice).
- They can **create money** (partnerships, jobs).

---

### How relationships connect to tools

- People share or recommend tools.
- Teams can use tools more effectively than one person.

---

### How relationships connect to Yin and Yang

- **Yin** = listening, patience, loyalty  
- **Yang** = speaking, action, collaboration  

Both are needed:
- **Yin builds trust.**
- **Yang builds progress.**

---

### Simple example

You want to start a project:
- A friend gives advice (knowledge)
- A mentor opens a door (opportunity)

Now your relationships create new value.

---

### First principles summary

**Relationships = trust connections that help people build together.**  
**They are social capital.**  
**Balance (Yin + Yang) = listen + act.**
