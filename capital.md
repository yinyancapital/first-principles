### Start with the basic truths
1. **People want to build things.**  
   That could be a business, a home, or a new idea.
2. **Building needs resources.**  
   Money, tools, time, and skills.
3. **Resources are limited.**  
   So how you use them matters.

---

### What is capital (first principles)?

**Capital** = **resources used to build or grow something.**

That can be:
- Money
- Tools
- Time
- Skills
- Knowledge

---

### First principles summary

**Capital = resources used to build.**
